Self-Disqualification Protocol

Application for Intentional Continuation

This process exists for your reflection. Nothing you enter will be evaluated, scored, or used to persuade you. Continue only if you are prepared to look directly at your own patterns.

  • You are not being evaluated and no one will review these scores.
  • The purpose is to help you decide whether to proceed.
  • Respond instinctively. There are no right answers.
  • You may stop at any time.
